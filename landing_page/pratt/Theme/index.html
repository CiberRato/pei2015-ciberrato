Project Description





What is the CiberRato?

CiberRato is a robotic competition organized by University of Aveiro which takes place in a simulation environment running in a network of computers. In this competition, there are teams composed by one or more participants and each team have a agent(robot) with software provided by them which control the movement of the virtual robots to accomplish the competition goal. The teams may use some languages, like python, C/C++, java, etc.


But what's the main goal of CiberRato?

Agents, playing as a team of five have the main goal: starting from their positions in the starting grid they must find and reach a beacon/cheese in the maze. They must have a team inside the beacon area and then make them return immediately to the start position. The final score depends on fulfilment of challenge goals, rules and on suffered penalties.


And what about our CiberRato project?

In our project we pretend enrich the environment of CiberRato simulation to place it efective and appealingly in Web. Participants must provide the software for his agent and in the day of the competition, agents will use the provided software. When the competition begins, participants can see it in live stream. When it's over, participants can watch the log of the competition saved automatically.


I want to participate, what should I do?

To participate, you must go to the menu bar above, click on "Login/Create Account".If you don't have account, you must create it. After this, you can do login to your CiberRato's home page. Thenceforth, you may able to participate in the competitions.


About Us


We are a group of students studying Integrated Master Computer and Telematics Engineering in University of Aveiro. We developed this project with help of the respective mentors described above in the section "Members and Team"

We decided develop this project because the environment of CiberRato was only available in University of Aveiro and we wanted to make it available for all universities and others schools. We developed it in a web platform for users not requiring the installation of a set of tools to run it.

This environment is also used in some disciplines of course and is necessary to improve it for students enjoy and can develop your work in a manner more simple and fast.
